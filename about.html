THE ARTIST

Precious Ugbo.

I am a Nigerian based digital illustrator living in Lagos.

I got inspired to create art proffessionally during the covid pandemic of 2020. Since then, i've sold numerous prints of my work and collaborated with several people and artist on personal pieces and music covers respectively.

My work is majorly influenced by being a black woman. And i enjoy depicting love, serenity and nature in my art.
